About Iron Maiden

Iron Maiden is a British heavy metal band founded in London in 1975 by bassist Steve Harris. Considered one of the most important and representative groups of the genre, they have sold over 100 million records worldwide, despite having received little support from mass media during most of their career. The band has based their success on directly reaching fans, recording high-quality albums, and delivering outstanding live performances.
